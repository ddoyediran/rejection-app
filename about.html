How it works?

You only have to ask people for things outside your comfort zone everyday - it is that simple. Stick to it you'll find yourself winning a lot more.

Ask people for things outside of your or their comfort zone

Then fill the form on Get Started with the question you ask and who you asked here

If your request was granted click "Accepted" or "Rejected" if you didn't get it. Note: Win = 1 point. Rejection = 10 points.

Then this app will keep track of your score and asking activities

Keep at it - you are on to something GREAT!
