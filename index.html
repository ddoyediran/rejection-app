Rejection Calculation App

The game has one rule:

You must be rejected by a human being at least once per day.

Why this app: It will help you to keep track and train yourself to be comfortable with Rejection

What you have to do: You only have to ask people for things outside your comfort zone everyday - it is that simple. Stick to it you'll find yourself winning a lot more.

How it works:

  1. Ask people for things outside of your or their comfort zone
  2. Then fill the form below with the question you ask and who you asked
  3. If your request was granted click "Accepted" or "Rejected" if you didn't get it. Note: Win = 1 point. Rejection = 10 points.
  4. Then this app will keep track of your score and asking activities
